    Public Opinion

    To address the questions of the correlation between the media and the public opinion, we chose to look at the number of tweets and if they were positives or negatives.
    We assumed that the tweets can give us a general idea on how the public see each candidate. In particular, we have collected 2000 tweets daily for each candidate and performed sentimental analysis. As for the media side, we used two media sources: speech/debate and articles on the newspapers.

    The content of the tweets

    Melenchon had the best coverage on April 17 and this is not a surprise since he had a huge and unusual meeting that day on the « quai de Seine. »
